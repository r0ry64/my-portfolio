About Me

My name is Edgar Rory Cobb, but everyone calls me Rory. I'm a 24 year old Mexican-Canadian man from Calgary, Alberta but I currently live in Guelph, Ontario.

My main interests are hip-hop music, combat sports (MMA & boxing), and retro gaming.

I worked in construction for a while and previously went to school for skilled trades. But after bad experiences and injuries, I have decided to switch career paths.

I have always been interested in programming since I was young. I grew up playing old DOS and Windows games, and I learned the basics of programming from creating my own mods and maps for those games.
